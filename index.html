Our History

Durham House of Pets is founded by a public animal fund since 2003. We are doing our best to save homeless pets, supply medical treatment and help them to find a new host. We're always here to help customers. We opened our first office in Durham NH. The team enriches the life of pets around the NH. Also we have a deeply cooperation with global animal fund and local community for taking care of pets.

Why Trust Us


1. We focus on developing pets management system.
2.Our team members are certified and have experience for many years.
3. We accept pets insurance.
4. Our pets undergo regular medical check-ups and we only sell healthy pets.
5. We provide on-site service.
