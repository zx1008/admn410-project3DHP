Store Policy

Financing Policy

We will match DHP.com and in store base price, for identical items purchased in a DHP store. We have membership club. Every dollar you spend will have points. Points can redeemed for our services and products. And we accept several payments as follows:

  • Visa/American Express/MasterCard/Discover/PayPal/GiftCard

Important Information:

The Gift Card can be used only to purchase merchandise and services from DHP.


Shipping

We provide free standard shipping over $49 with 2-days delivery. If you have Durham House of Pets(DHP)membership card, free next day shipping is available with no specific spending amount.


Choose store pickup for your online order and we will send you an email as soon as possible when it's ready for pickup.


Return

Not 100% happy with your purchase? Now there are some easier and faster way to bring items back within 60 days order.


In Store


Firstly, please bring your receipt. Secondly, find the service desk in store. Then join the express lane, click scan on the home screen and drop your item. We will return your money back with store gift card or your original credit card.


By Mail

Ship it back for free. It's as easy as logging in to your account & printing a return label. Please note: Returned items must be in their original condition.
